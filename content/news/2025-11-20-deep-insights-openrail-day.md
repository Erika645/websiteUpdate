OpenRail Association gives at the OpenRail Day a deep insight to an variety of open source projects.
These are the projects:
* OSRD (Open Source Railway Designer)
* LibLRS (Library for Linear Reference Systems)
* NGE (Netzgrafik Editor)
* RCM OSS (Rail Condition Monitoring)
* DAC (Digital Automatic Coupling)
